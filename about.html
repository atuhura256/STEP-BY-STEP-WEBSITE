About Step by Step Schools

Step by Step Primary and Secondary Schools were founded on the principles of academic excellence and strong moral discipline. We have grown from a small institution to a group of schools that serves hundreds of students across Uganda.

Our curriculum is designed to balance the national academic standards with practical skills in ICT, music, and sports, ensuring that every student discovers their unique talent.
